Prof. Dr. Mahmudul Hasan Siddiqi Shiblee ,a beacon in the field of Ophthalmology ,stands as a distinguished figure specializing in Vitro retina ,Retinopathy of Prematurity and Phaco emulsification Surgery.


With an illustrious academic journey ,Prof. Shiblee Siddiqi holds a plethora of qualifications, including MBBS , Diploma in Ophthalmology (DO), observership in cornea and Refrective surgery in Singapore National Eye Center (SNEC). His commitment to excellence is further exemplified by Long term Fellowship in Vitreo Retina at national Institute Of Ophthalmology where he honed his skills and passion for providing specialized care to all Retina patients in the field of Medical as well as surgical retina ,Retinopathy of Prematurity in New born. Dr. Shiblee is not only renowned for his expertise in treating Retina cases but also for his long history of doing cataract surgery for 20 years including Phacoemulsification surgery. His comprehensive approach to patient care reflects his dedication to the visual well being of individuals across all age groups.


Beyond his clinical practice , Prof. Shiblee Siddiqi actively engages himself as Executive Editor of President Abdul Hamid Medical Journal (PAHMJ) . He has presented research papers in both national and international conferences , contributed to training programs and workshops and published articles in esteemed journals, solidifying his status as thought leader in the field. He is the Examiner of Final MBBS examinations since 2012.


As a professor of Ophthalmology , Dr Shiblee Siddiqi plays a pivotal role in shaping the future of eye care professionals . His commitment to education is evident his contributions to the growth of ophthalmic knowledge, particularly in the specialized domain of Vitreo Retina from neonates to elderly patients.He remains dedicated to staying abreast of the latest advancements in ophthalmology ,ensuring that his patients receive cutting edge eye care.
